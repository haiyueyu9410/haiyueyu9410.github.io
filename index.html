About

I am a Lead Applied Scientist at Snowflake, building to end-to-end machine learning models and GenAI applications.

I hold a Ph.D. in Financial Economics from Princeton University, where I was fortunate to be advised by Motohiro Yogo. My thesis, Institutional Investors and Liquidity in the Corporate Bond Market, examines institutional investors and liquidity in the corporate bond market.

Previously worked at Two Sigma as a quantitative researcher, focusing on global macro alpha research.

Research Interests

  • GenAI applications in finance
